Restrictions for Travel to (Canada)

Restrictions:

  1. Entering Yukon as a fully vaccinated travellers

  2. Self-isolation and other requirements



Proof of vanccination

As of November 30, 2021, all travellers 12 years of age plus 4 months and older boarding domestic flights and trains need to be fully-vaccinated.

If you’re 12 years of age or older you must have proof that you’ve received 2 doses of a COVID-19 vaccination. You cannot access a setting that requires proof of vaccination if you’ve had only 1 dose or are not vaccinated.


*More details can be found at Things to know before coming to the Yukon.

Self-isolation requirements

Travellers arriving in the Yukon are no longer required to complete a self-isolation period.


*More details can be found at Things to know before coming to the Yukon.

Mask requirements

Anyone aged 5 and over must wear a mask:
  • in any indoor public place, including retail and service businesses;
  • in private vehicles where the occupants are from more than 1 household; and
  • in outdoor public places when people cannot maintain 2 metres of distancing.

*More details can be found at Mask wearing - Government of Yukon.
