Restrictions for Travel to (Canada)

Restrictions:

  1. Entering Northwest Territories as a fully vaccinated travellers

  2. Self-isolation and other requirements



Proof of vanccination

Non-residents or those who received their vaccine outside the NWT will be required to provide proof of vaccination upon request. It is recommended that these travellers have proof of vaccination from the jurisdiction in which it was received before travelling.

All travellers entering the NWT are required to complete a self-isolation plan, to show their vaccination status. We recommend you complete your self-isolation plan two weeks before you will arrive in the NWT.

  • Complete your self-isolation plan.
  • After you have completely your self-isolation plan, you will receive an email approving your plan and providing you with information about whether you need to self-isolate and how to complete your symptom checks.

*More details can be found at Self-isolation plan.

Self-isolation requirements

All travellers need to submit a Self-Isolation Plan regardless of vaccination status.

No self-isolation for fully vaccinated travellers and household members (regardless of vaccination status) who did not travel, provided they are not sharing accommodations with travellers who are not fully vaccinated.

Fully vaccinated individuals travelling with unvaccinated individuals will be required to isolate upon return to the NWT for the duration of the least vaccinated traveller if they live in the same household.

Fully vaccinated travellers will be able to return directly to small communities, but they must complete a Day 1 and Day 8 COVID-19 test.

*More details can be found at Self-Isolation.

Addtional testing

  • Fully vaccinated travellers entering small NWT communities will require a day 1 test and a day 8 test.
  • Partially vaccinated travellers will require a day 8 negative test if they are travelling to a small community after completing their self-isolation in a larger centre.
  • Unvaccinated travellers will require a day 10 negative test if they are travelling to a small community after completing their self-isolation in a larger centre.

Small communities are any NWT community that does not include the hub communities of Yellowknife, Hay River, Inuvik, and Fort Smith, as well as Fort Simpson and Norman Wells.


*More details can be found at GNWT's Response to COVID-19.

Mask requirements

COVID-19 spreads in the air far more easily than by surface. To protect yourself and others, wear a non-medical mask or face covering:

  • Anytime it is required by a business or service
  • Indoors in crowded places
  • Indoors in a location with poor ventilation
  • Indoors where there is shouting, singing, playing music (especially wind instruments) or exercise (if applicable)
  • Outdoors in crowded spaces when it is difficult to maintain physical distance.

*More details can be found at Masks - GNWT's Response to COVID-19.
